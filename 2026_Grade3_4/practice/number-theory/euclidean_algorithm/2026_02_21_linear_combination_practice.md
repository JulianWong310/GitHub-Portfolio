| **Q3**   | **B**  | Rule 2 specifically covers the subtraction of two multiples. |

| **Q5**   | **B**  | Since $y = (x+y) - x$, if $7$ divides both parts, it divides $y$. |
| **Q6**   | **C**  | $12(2) + 18(-1) = 24 - 18 = 6$. |
| **Q7**   | **D**  | All are linear combinations ($qx+ry$) of $24$ and $36$. |
